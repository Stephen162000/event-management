    Wowevent is a digitized attendance & identification management system that
    allows event organizers and hosts to efficiently record guest attendance.
    The system can be used to keep track of activities of attendees and guests at summits,
    conferences, and other important events. Wowevents gives you the access to know
    guest time-in, time-out, phone number, IDs etc. Administrators gain access to
    dashboards with bulk SMS on-the-go features and many more.
